About Me

Hi, I’m Irem — a 3rd-year Computational Engineering student with a minor in Computer Science at Aalto University. I am passionate about machine learning, robotics, and building solutions that bridge technology and human impact. My academic coursework combines Machine Learning, C++, and Data Structures with hands-on engineering experience, from designing competition-winning robots at VEX Robotics to optimizing material models using Machine Learning and CPFEM simulations. Beyond the lab, I thrive on leadership: co-founding UniPath to guide students abroad, organizing multiple TEDx events, and being named a finalist for EY NextGen Women for my strategic thinking. I'm currently writing my thesis on "AI-driven Transformation in Structural Engineering" and will be starting my master's degree in September 2025 on Machine Learning, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at Aalto University.
